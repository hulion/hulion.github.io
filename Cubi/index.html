What is CUBI?

A New Way for Children to Communicate. Cubi is a wearable device in a watch form which packed with multiple functionalities. It can tell time, send and receive voice messages, build-in voice changers, instant emergency alert feature and works as a GPS locator to ease parent’s greatest fear.

Who’s Cubi for?

Crafted for children from age 3 ~ 10.

*Battery life on stand-by will be 24-hours and continuous using is estimated at around 5-hours.

Cubi is a communicator for kids. The fundamental idea is to build-up and strengthen the connection within family. Allows children to express their feeling freely with people they love. 

More so, communicating between peers has been a huge part of our daily life. We adults gets to stay informed with people we know and people we love at any given time. As for children, especially when they are not allow to carry a phone yet, it’s not as easy as they wish it would be.

Thus, we’ve created Cubi. That is easy to use, comfortable to wear and packed with all the necessary tools to serve their everyday needs.

Last but not least, Cubi comes equipped with various protecting functions to ease parents worries. Plus it also comes with several marvelous features made especially for children.

Communication - Stay In Touch at All Time.

Cubi can send and receive voice messages. Allows children to safely venture on their own but have the ability to report back any obstacles or finding on their young journey. The beauty of it is that children can choose their desire timing when sending or receiving to avoid interruption. Both parents and children can also repeatedly listen to any voice messages on their Cubi and smartphone.

    GPS locator. Arriving / Leaving Safety Zone

    Parents can not only be more involve with children’s daily activity but also gets to monitor children’s whereabout at all time.

    Emergency Alert Button

    Press and hold the Emergency Alert button for 3 seconds, and parent’s phone will receive the emergency message and the current location of the children.

    Co-Guard

    Parents can give permission to other family members or trusted friends to all pair up with Cubi on making sure each and everyone you trust can watch your children together.

    Vibration Mode

    There is an on-off switch on the side of the Cubi to turn on and off the vibration mode.

    Flash Light

    Allows children to explore the world freely when they always carries a torch with them.
